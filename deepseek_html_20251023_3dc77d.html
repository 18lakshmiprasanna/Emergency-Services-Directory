Emergency Preparedness Tips

First Aid Basics

Learn CPR and basic first aid. Keep a first aid kit at home and in your car.

Fire Safety

Install smoke detectors, have fire extinguishers, and create an evacuation plan.

Power Outage

Keep flashlights, batteries, and a battery-powered radio ready.

Natural Disasters

Know evacuation routes and have an emergency kit with 3 days of supplies.
